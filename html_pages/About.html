Our Vision

To be the leading centre of excellence, to provide the best firstaid treatment available. To provide first and immediate assistance to any person suffering from either a minor or serious illness or injury,to preserve life, prevent the condition from worsening, and promote recovery.

Our Values

Respect

We respect the dignity of each individual at all times. Every decision made by each individual is fully respected.

One of the most important values is teamwork, we recognize, embrace and capitalize on the unique talents of everyone. Teamwork creates better result.

Compassion

We demonstrate our sincere compassion to all. We try our possible best to provide the best treatment to others
